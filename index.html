Power-Up Honey Glazed Beef

PREP 10 MIN
|
COOK 15 MIN
|
SERVES 1
|
DIFFICULTY EASY

Ingredients

  • 🥩 220g Flank Steak, thin sliced
  • 🍚 150g Jasmine Rice (Cooked Weight)
  • 🍯 1 tbsp Organic Raw Honey
  • 🧄 2 cloves Garlic, crushed fine
  • 醬 1 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce / Tamari
  • 🫛 100g Sugar Snap Peas
  • 🌱 1 tsp Toasted Sesame Seeds

Nutrition

590
Calories
46g
Protein
62g
Carbs
14g
Fat

Method

01
Whisk the glaze base: In a small dynamic prep bowl, rapidly blend your raw honey, crushed garlic, and dark soy sauce together until smooth and integrated.
02
Flash sear beef: Fire a non-stick wok over maximum heat. Flash fry the thin steak slices for 3 minutes until caramelized edges develop perfectly.
03
Introduce greens & sauce: Drop snap peas straight into the pan workspace, instantly pouring the honey glaze evenly over your protein matrix. Toss aggressively for 90 seconds.
04
Serve & finish: Stack over warm jasmine rice baseline layers. Top off your complete macro assembly with a scattering of toasted sesame seeds.
💡 Chaz Fitness Tip

Flank steak is an excellent source of lean protein and zinc, vital for muscle recovery. Searing it rapidly preserves internal juices without requiring excess oils, locking in clean macro-performance.
